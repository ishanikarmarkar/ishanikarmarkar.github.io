Ishani Karmarkar

I am currently a PhD student at Stanford University. My research primarily focuses on problems at the intersection of optimization, machine learning, and algorithm design. I am very fortunate to be advised by Aaron Sidford and Ellen Vitercik.

Previously, I earned a BS in Applied and Computational Mathematics with a Minor in Computer Science from the California Institute of Technology (Caltech), where I did undergraduate research with Bamdad Hosseini and Andrew Stuart.
